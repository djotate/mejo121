Dylan Tate

Bio

Hi! My name is Dylan "Dilly-Jo" Tate, and I am a student in the Hussman School of Journalism and Media at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. I am a gaming and esports journalist with a love for Nintendo video games, including Super Smash Bros., Pokemon, and many more. Currently, I write news stories for Daily Esports and Nintendo Enthusiast. You can check out my full portfolio here.

Fun Facts

  • Not only do I report on competitive Smash Bros., but I also compete in it! I am currently ranked No. 10 in Chapel Hill, NC for Super Smash Bros. Ultimate.
